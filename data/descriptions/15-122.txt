Office Hours
[
iCal format
]
Office hour rules:
OH on the day of a programming deadline is
conceptual office hours
:
    no help will be
  provided on the programming homework due that same day —
  manage your time carefully!
Office hours are not where you do homework with a TA around in
  case you get stuck: go to office hours if you've thought about your
  problem for some time and are still stuck.
